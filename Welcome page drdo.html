DRDO CONTRIBUTION TO DEFENCE TECHNOLOGY

HOUNARABLE MENTION

Agni Missile

The Agni series comprises ballistic missiles with varying ranges, from short to intercontinental. These missiles are a key part of India's strategic deterrence capability.

Arjun Main Battle Tank

The Arjun is an advanced main battle tank with superior firepower, high mobility, and excellent protection capabilities. It features a 120mm rifled gun and Kanchan armor.

Light Combat Aircraft (LCA) Tejas

Tejas is a lightweight, multi-role supersonic fighter aircraft designed for air combat and offensive air support roles. It has advanced avionics, a fly-by-wire control system, and composite materials for reduced weight.

NETRA AEW&C System

NETRA is an airborne early warning and control system that provides airborne surveillance, tracking, and command and control capabilities. It is mounted on a modified Embraer ERJ 145 platform.

Rustom UAV

Rustom is a series of unmanned aerial vehicles designed for surveillance and reconnaissance missions. It can be used for target acquisition and artillery fire correction.

Advanced Towed Artillery Gun System (ATAGS)

ATAGS is an indigenously developed 155mm artillery gun with a range of over 48 km, featuring automated loading and aiming systems.
